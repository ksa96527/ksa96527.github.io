Anderson Farms is a local family owned farm in Thomaston, Georgia. The responsibilities of Anderson Farms are routinely carried out by husband and wife Mike and Sara Anderson. We offer many things to sell. We offer hatching eggs from many different species of bird. Both fresh chicken and fresh quail eggs are collected and washed daily. They are ready for consumption at time of buying. A numerous amount of common farm animals are raised on Anderson Farms from newborns to adults. They are fed organic feed, and their enclosures are cleaned daily.
